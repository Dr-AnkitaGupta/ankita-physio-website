Back Pain Treatment in Gwalior

Physiotherapy care for lower back pain, sciatica symptoms, posture strain, stiffness and pain that affects sitting, standing, walking or daily work.

Benefits

  • Reduce pain and stiffness.
  • Improve posture, core strength and flexibility.
  • Support safer movement at work and home.
  • Lower the chance of repeated pain episodes.

Symptoms

  • Lower back pain while sitting or bending.
  • Pain spreading to hip or leg.
  • Morning stiffness or muscle spasm.
  • Difficulty lifting, walking or standing long.

Treatment Process

  • Assessment of posture, movement and pain pattern.
  • Manual therapy and pain-relief techniques where suitable.
  • Stretching, strengthening and core exercises.
  • Ergonomic and home-care guidance.

FAQs

Can physiotherapy help back pain?

Yes, physiotherapy can help reduce pain, improve movement and build strength when treatment is matched to the cause of pain.

Do you treat sciatica symptoms?

Physiotherapy can support sciatica-related pain with assessment, mobility work, nerve-friendly exercises and posture guidance.

Can back pain treatment be done at home?

Yes, home physiotherapy is available in Gwalior for patients who prefer treatment at home.
