Benefits

  • Improve posture, mobility and comfort.
  • Support antenatal and postnatal recovery.
  • Regain strength safely after delivery.
  • Get guided home exercises for long-term function.

Symptoms

  • Back pain, pelvic discomfort or posture strain.
  • Weakness after pregnancy or surgery.
  • Difficulty returning to daily activity.
  • Stiffness, fatigue or reduced mobility.

Treatment Process

  • Comfort-focused assessment and goal setting.
  • Gentle mobility, posture and strengthening work.
  • Breathing, core and recovery exercises where suitable.
  • Home program and progress review.

FAQs

What is women's health physiotherapy?

It is physiotherapy focused on women's pain, posture, antenatal, postnatal and recovery needs.

Can physiotherapy help after delivery?

Yes, postnatal physiotherapy can help rebuild strength, improve posture and support safe return to activity.

Can I book a home session?
